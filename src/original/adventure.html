Adventures in Bhutan

Discover thrilling experiences in the Land of the Thunder Dragon

Popular Adventure Ideas in Bhutan

Snowman Trek

One of the most challenging treks in the world, crossing 11 passes over 4,500m.

  • Duration: 25-27 days
  • Max Elevation: 5,320m
  • Difficulty: Challenging
Tiger's Nest Monastery Trek

Iconic cliffside monastery trek offering spiritual and scenic experiences.

  • Duration: 1 day
  • Elevation: 3,120m
  • Difficulty: Moderate
Punakha River Rafting

White-water rafting adventures through scenic valleys and past the majestic Punakha Dzong.

  • Duration: 2-3 hours
  • Rapids: Grade II-III
  • Difficulty: Moderate
Bumthang Valley Camping

Immersive camping experience in the spiritual heartland of Bhutan.

  • Duration: 2-3 days
  • Location: Bumthang Valley
  • Difficulty: Easy
Mountain Biking Tours

Scenic trails through pine forests and ancient monasteries.

  • Duration: 1-5 days
  • Various Routes
  • Difficulty: Varied
Rock Climbing - The Nose

Bhutan's premier outdoor climbing location with routes for all levels.

  • Duration: Half/Full day
  • Various Grades
  • All Skill Levels
Phobjikha Valley Bird Watching

Observe rare black-necked cranes in their natural habitat.

  • Best: Nov-Mar
  • Multiple Species
  • Difficulty: Easy
Dochula Pass Motorcycle Tour

Epic motorcycle journey across Bhutan's most scenic mountain passes.

  • Duration: 3-7 days
  • Multiple Routes
  • Difficulty: Moderate
Eastern Bhutan Kayaking

Paddle through pristine rivers in Bhutan's less-explored eastern region.

  • Duration: 1-3 days
  • Various Grades
  • Moderate-Advanced
