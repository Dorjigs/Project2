Welcome to Bhutan

Experience the magic of the last Himalayan kingdom.

Nestled in the eastern Himalayas between India and China, Bhutan is a land of stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and spiritual significance. Known for its commitment to Gross National Happiness over Gross National Product, Bhutan offers travelers a unique glimpse into a country that prioritizes cultural preservation and environmental conservation.

Our comprehensive guide helps you discover ancient fortresses (dzongs), Buddhist monasteries perched on cliffs, pristine landscapes, and vibrant festivals that bring Bhutan's cultural heritage to life.

Essential Travel Tips

Planning a trip to Bhutan requires knowledge of its unique travel policies and requirements.

  • All tourists must obtain a visa and book through licensed tour operators
  • Daily tourist tariff covers accommodations, meals, guide, and internal transport
  • Best seasons to visit: Spring (Mar-May) and Autumn (Sep-Nov)
  • Pack for varying climates and altitudes
